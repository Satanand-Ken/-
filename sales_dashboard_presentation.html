💡 Business Insights Delivered

📦

Product Performance

Clothing dominates at 62.62% of sales, followed by Electronics (20.55%) and Furniture (16.83%). Printers lead profitability in sub-categories.

💳

Payment Preferences

COD leads at 44%, followed by UPI at 21%. Multi-channel payment support is crucial for customer convenience.

📍

Geographic Distribution

Indore (71 orders), Mumbai (67 orders), and Chandigarh (30 orders) are top-performing cities, indicating strong urban market presence.

📉

Order Quality

64.73% orders are profitable while 35.27% are loss-making. Focus needed on improving margin on loss-generating orders.

📅

Seasonal Trends

December shows highest profitability spike. January-March and December show strong performance, indicating seasonal opportunities.
